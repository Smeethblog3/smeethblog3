About Olamide

  • Olamide Gbenga Adedeji was born in Bariga, Lagos State
  • Date Of Birth:15th March 1989
  • He is the second of Pa Adedeji's three children with Ronke Osisanya Adedeji, his second wife.
  • TemmyGold and Eniola Olamilekan (also known as DJ Enimoney) are Olamide's two Siblings

Olamide's Top Projects

  • Rapsodi: This is Olamide's first album, which was released under ID Cabasa's record label, Coded Tunes Records.
  • 999 EP: This is Olamide's 10th studio project, which features nine tracks and includes collaborations with Phyno, Rhatti, Show, Cheque, Jayboi, Sosa-E and Jackwillz.
  • Carpe Diem: This album features the song "Shilalo," which is a smooth love rhythm that showcases the synergy between Olamide and Phyno.
  • UY Scuti": This album is a collaboration with Phyno and features a Dancehall-inspired love song called "Somebody."
  • Unruly: This is Olamide's 10th studio album, which shows off his ability to experiment with different sounds and collaborate with other artists.

Olamide, a leading figure in the Nigerian music industry, has achieved numerous significant milestones throughout his career:

  1. Album Releases:

    2. Olamide has released numerous studio albums, including "Rapsodi" (2011), "YBNL" (2012), "Baddest Guy Ever Liveth" (2013), "Street OT" (2014), "Eyan Mayweather" (2015), "The Glory" (2016), "Lagos Nawa" (2017), "Carpe Diem" (2020), "UY Scuti" (2021), and "Unruly" (2023)

  2. Awards and Nominations:

    3. He has received several awards, including multiple Nigeria Entertainment Awards, The Headies awards, and City People Entertainment Awards. His impact has also been recognized internationally with nominations such as being named to Billboard's 2023 International Power Players list

  3. Label Founder:

    4. In 2012, Olamide founded the YBNL Nation record label, which has been instrumental in launching the careers of artists like Fireboy DML, Adekunle Gold, and Asake. YBNL Nation signed a distribution deal with Empire in 2020, further expanding its reach

  4. Major Collaborations:

    5. Olamide has collaborated with various high-profile artists and producers, enhancing his versatility and influence in the music scene. Notable collaborations include works with Phyno, Wizkid, Burna Boy, and international artists

  5. Philanthropy and Influence:

    6. Beyond music, Olamide has been active in philanthropy and community support, using his platform to influence and support various causes and emerging talents in Nigeria

Olamide's career is marked by his consistent output, innovation, and support for new artists, cementing his status as one of the most influential figures in African music.
