Rema's Personal Achievements

Rema, whose real name is Divine Ikubor, has achieved significant milestones in his career, particularly in 2023, marking him as one of the leading figures in the global Afrobeats scene.

  1. Chart Success:

    2. Rema's single "Calm Down" became the highest-charting Nigerian song on the Billboard Hot 100, reaching number 3. This song also achieved the status of the most streamed African song on Spotify, surpassing 1 billion streams

  2. MTV Awards:

    3. At the 2023 MTV Europe Music Awards, Rema won the award for Best Afrobeats Artist

  3. Headies Awards:

    4. He won Best Male Artist, Digital Artist of the Year, and African Artist of the Year at the 2023 Headies Awards

  4. International Tours and Performances:

    5. Rema's music has found a unique audience in India, leading to sold-out tours across major cities such as Delhi, Mumbai, and Hyderabad. He has also performed at prestigious events like the Ballon D'Or

  5. Other Accolades:

    6. Rema received several other recognitions in 2023, including having the most viewed Nigerian music video on YouTube and achieving the longest-charting African song on the Billboard US Afrobeats Songs chart. Additionally, he won Best Male Artist at the 16th Headies Awards

These achievements highlight Rema's significant impact on the global music stage and his role in promoting Afrobeats worldwide.
