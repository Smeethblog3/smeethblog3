About Davido

  • Full Name:David Adedeji Adeleke OON
  • Date Of Birth:(born November 21, 1992)
  • Davido is a Nigerian singer, songwriter and
    record producer
  • He is regarded as one of the most important
    Afrobeats artists of the 21st century
  • He is credited alongside Burna Boy and Wizkid for popularizing the genre globally

Davido's Top Projects

  • Omo Baba Olowo: The Genesis" (2012) - Debut album with a mix of positive and negative reviews, but still earned award nominations.
  • A Good Time" (2019) - Critically acclaimed album with top-tier features and exploration of various sonic palettes.
  • A Better Time" (2020) - Features a wide range of producers and guest appearances from Nicki Minaj, Nas, and others.
  • Timeless" (2023) - Explores amapiano and other styles, featuring collaborations with Skepta, Angelique Kidjo, and others.

These projects showcase Davido's growth and evolution as an artist, as well as his ability to collaborate and experiment with different styles.

Davido, the Nigerian Afrobeats superstar, has an impressive array of personal achievements and accolades. Here are some highlights from his career:

    • The Headies: Davido has won several awards, including Artiste of the Year (2018), Song of the Year for "If" (2018), and Best Pop Single for "Aye" (2014)

    • MTV Africa Music Awards: He has been awarded multiple times, including Best Male and Best Collaboration
    • Nigeria Entertainment Awards: Multiple wins including Best New Act of the Year (2012) and Hottest Single of the Year for "Dami Duro" (2012)
  2. Global Recognition:
    • Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards: Nominated for Favorite African Star in 2018
    • AFRIMA (All Africa Music Awards): Multiple nominations including Artist of the Year and Best Male West Africa
  3. Chart-Topping Albums and Singles:

    4. His albums, such as "Omo Baba Olowo," "A Good Time," and "A Better Time," have topped various charts. The album "Timeless," released in 2023, received widespread acclaim and contributed significantly to his career milestones in that year

  4. Major Collaborations and Performances:

    5. Davido has collaborated with international artists like Chris Brown, Nicki Minaj, and Meek Mill. His influence in integrating Afrobeat into mainstream Western music has been widely acknowledged

  5. Philanthropy and Personal Milestones:

    6. Beyond music, Davido is known for his philanthropic efforts. He has donated to various causes, including educational support and healthcare

  6. Recent Achievements in 2023:

    7. Davido's latest album "Timeless" was highly successful, receiving Grammy nominations and extensive press coverage. His performances across global stages and viral moments further solidified his status as a leading figure in Afrobeat
