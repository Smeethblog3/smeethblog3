About Burna Boy

  • Full Name:Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu MFR
  • Date Of Birth:(born 2 July 1991)
  • Burna Boy, is a Nigerian singer, songwriter and record producer
  • He rose to stardom in 2012 after releasing "Like to Party", the lead single from his debut studio album L.I.F.E

Burna Boy's Top Projects

  • I Told Them: This album is a bouquet of hits and enjoyable melodies.
  • Love Damini: A grammmy award features collaborations with artists like Ed Sheeran and J Balvin.
  • Twice as Tall: This album became his highest-charting project so far and debuted at number 54 on the Billboard 200.
  • African Giant: This album won the Album of the Year at the 2019 All Africa Music Awards and was nominated for Best World Music Album at the 62nd Annual Grammy Awards.
  • American Dream: This album features Burna Boy on 21 Savage's track "Just Like Me" and marks the rapper’s first solo album since 2018.
  • I Feel It: This track marked Jon Bellion's official comeback.

Burna Boy, one of Nigeria's most acclaimed musical talents, has a substantial list of achievements throughout his career. Here is a detailed summary of his major accomplishments:

  1. Grammy Awards
    • 2021: Won Best Global Music Album for "Twice as Tall"
    • 2020: Nominated for Best World Music Album for "African Giant".
  2. BET Awards
    • 2019: Won Best International Act.
    • 2020: Won Best International Act.
  3. Billboard Achievements
    • Topped the Billboard US Afrobeats Artists chart in 2023
    • African Giant" peaked at number 16 on the UK Albums Chart and number 104 on the Billboard 200
  4. MTV Awards
    • 2019: Won Best African Act at the MTV Europe Music Awards
    • 2019: Won African Artist of the Year at the Soundcity MVP Awards Festival
  5. Headies Awards
    • 2019: Best Performer and Best Collabo.
    • 2020: Artist of the Year and Best Recording of the Year.
  6. Collaborations And Features
    • Featured on Beyoncé's "The Lion King: The Gift" album on the track "Ja Ara E".
    • Collaborated with artists like Stormzy, Fall Out Boy, and Sam Smith
  7. Tours And Performances
    • Sold out the O2 Academy Brixton in 2018.
    • Performed at Coachella in 2019.
    • Conducted a successful global tour promoting "African Giant".
  8. Other Awards
    • Soundcity MVP Awards: Multiple wins including African Artiste of the Year and Song of the Year for "Last Last"
    • MOBO Awards: Best International Act in 2020 and Best African Act.

Burna Boy's impact on the music industry extends beyond his awards, with numerous hit singles, successful albums, and sold-out concerts worldwide. His work continues to shape the global perception of Afrobeats and African music.
